The IRS Electronic Tax Administration Advisory Committee (ETAAC) released its 2026 annual report (IR-2026-77) containing 18 recommendations, six of them directed to Congress. The report covers six priority areas including technology and data sharing, sustained IRS funding, AI and human-centered design, digital filing and payments, tax simplification and outreach, and fraud prevention and preparer regulation.
